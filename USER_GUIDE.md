## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create an Account

1. **Visit the homepage** at your DoGoods URL
2. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top-right corner
3. **Fill in your information**:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Location (city/area)
4. **Click "Create Account"**
5. **Check your email** for a verification link from Supabase
6. **Click the verification link** to activate your account
7. **Log in** with your email and password

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. **Click your profile icon** in the top-right corner
2. **Select "Profile"** from the dropdown
3. **Add optional information**:
   - Profile photo (upload from your computer)
   - Phone number
   - Address details
   - Bio/description
4. **Click "Save Changes"**

## Finding Food

### Browse Available Food

1. **Click "Find Food"** in the main navigation
2. **View all available listings** on the map and list view
3. **Filter by**:
   - Food category (produce, bakery, dairy, pantry items)
   - Location (near you)
   - Availability status (available now)

### Search for Specific Items

1. **Use the search bar** at the top of the Find Food page
2. **Type keywords** like "bread," "vegetables," or "dairy"
3. **Browse results** that match your search

### Claim Food

1. **Click on a food listing** you're interested in
2. **Review the details**:
   - Food description and quantity
   - Pickup location
   - Available dates/times
   - Photos
3. **Click "Claim Food"** button
4. **Fill out the claim form**:
   - Pickup date and time
   - Number of people this will feed
   - Optional: Number of students/school staff (if applicable)
   - Optional: Message to the donor
5. **Click "Submit Claim"**
6. **Wait for approval** from the food donor
7. **Check your notifications** for approval status
8. **Pick up the food** at the agreed time and location

### View Your Claims

1. **Go to "My Dashboard"** from the profile menu
2. **Click "My Claims"** tab
3. **See all your food claims**:
   - Pending (waiting for approval)
   - Approved (ready for pickup)
   - Completed (picked up)
   - Cancelled

## Sharing Food

### Create a Food Listing

1. **Click "Share Food"** in the main navigation
2. **Fill out the listing form**:

   **Basic Information:**
   - Food title (e.g., "Fresh vegetables from garden")
   - Description (what's included, condition, etc.)
   - Quantity (e.g., "5 lbs," "10 servings")
   - Food category (produce, bakery, dairy, pantry items)

   **Location & Pickup:**
   - Pickup address
   - City
   - Postal code
   - Available from (date)
   - Available until (date)

   **Photos:**
   - Upload up to 3 photos of the food
   - Click "Browse" or drag and drop images

   **Additional Details:**
   - Special instructions (e.g., "Ring doorbell," "Use side entrance")
   - Dietary information (allergens, vegetarian, etc.)

3. **Click "Share Food"** to submit
4. **Wait for admin approval** (usually within 24 hours)
5. **Receive notification** when approved

### Manage Claims on Your Listings

1. **Go to "My Dashboard"** ‚Üí **"My Listings"** tab
2. **Click on a listing** to view claims
3. **Review claim requests**:
   - See who claimed the food
   - View pickup time requested
   - Read messages from claimants
4. **Approve or reject claims**:
   - Click "Approve" to accept the claim
   - Click "Reject" if you can't fulfill it
   - Add a message explaining your decision
5. **Mark as picked up** after successful pickup:
   - Click "Mark as Completed"
   - This updates your impact metrics

### Edit or Delete Listings

1. **Go to "My Listings"**
2. **Click the listing** you want to modify
3. **Choose an action**:
   - **Edit**: Update details, photos, or availability
   - **Delete**: Remove the listing permanently
   - **Mark as Unavailable**: Temporarily hide from search

## Admin Features (Admin Users Only)

### Access Admin Dashboard

1. **Log in as admin user**
2. **Profile Icon** ‚Üí **"Admin Dashboard"**
3. **View admin sections**:
   - Food Listings Management
   - User Management
   - Claims Review
   - Impact Data Entry
   - Reports & Analytics

### Approve Food Listings

1. **Admin Dashboard** ‚Üí **"Pending Listings"**
2. **Review listing details**:
   - Food description and photos
   - User information
   - Location and pickup details
3. **Approve or Reject**:
   - Click "Approve" to make it visible
   - Click "Reject" if it violates guidelines
   - Add rejection reason if rejecting
4. **Listing goes live** after approval

### Manage Impact Data

1. **Admin Dashboard** ‚Üí **"Impact Data Entry"**
2. **Add new impact metrics**:
   - Fill in the green row at top
   - Enter data for all columns:
     - Date
     - Food Saved from Waste (lb)
     - Food Provided (lb)
     - People Helped
     - Schools Served
     - Non-Profits Helped
     - Total Meals Provided
     - Notes
   - Click the **+** button to save
3. **Edit existing data**: Click any cell to edit, changes auto-save
4. **Delete entries**: Click trash icon on row
5. **Export data**: Click "Export CSV" button

### Manage Slideshow (Homepage Hero)

1. **Visit homepage** while logged in as admin
2. **Click "Manage Slides"** button (top-right of slideshow)
3. **Add new slide**:
   - Enter image URL (from Unsplash, Pexels, or your own hosting)
   - Add optional caption
   - Click "Add Slide"
4. **Remove slide**:
   - Find slide in list
   - Click trash icon
   - Confirm deletion
5. **Slides appear immediately** in rotation

**Finding Image URLs:**
- Visit [Unsplash.com](https://unsplash.com)
- Search for food/community images
- Right-click image ‚Üí "Copy image address"
- Paste URL into slideshow manager

### Edit Impact Story Page

1. **Visit Impact Story page** as admin
2. **Click "Edit Mode"** button (bottom-right)
3. **Click any text** to edit:
   - Hero title and subtitle
   - Featured content sections
   - Story titles and quotes
   - Gallery descriptions
4. **Edit toolbar appears** at top-right:
   - Click "Save Changes" when done
   - Click "Cancel" to revert
5. **Changes appear immediately** for all users

### Manage Users

1. **Admin Dashboard** ‚Üí **"Users"**
2. **View all registered users**
3. **Actions available**:
   - View user details
   - Make user admin (set `is_admin = true` in database)
   - Suspend user account
   - Delete user (with confirmation)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: "Invalid email or password"
- **Solution**: Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- **Check**: Email is verified (check spam folder for verification email)
- **Try**: Clear browser cache and cookies

### Images Won't Upload

**Problem**: Upload fails or shows error
- **Check file size**: Maximum 5MB per image
- **Check format**: Only JPG, PNG, GIF allowed
- **Try**: Compress image using online tools
- **Try**: Different browser or incognito mode

### Food Listing Not Showing

**Problem**: Can't see your listing in search
- **Check status**: Must be "Approved" by admin
- **Check dates**: Available dates must be current/future
- **Wait**: Admin approval can take up to 24 hours
- **Refresh**: Clear browser cache

### Claims Not Working

**Problem**: Can't submit claim or approval
- **Check**: You're logged in
- **Check**: Listing is still available
- **Check**: You haven't already claimed this item
- **Try**: Refresh page and try again

### Profile Photo Not Updating

**Problem**: Photo doesn't change after upload
- **Wait**: Give it a few seconds to process
- **Refresh**: Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
- **Check**: File is under 5MB
- **Try**: Different image format

### Notifications Not Received

**Problem**: Missing email notifications
- **Check spam folder**: May be filtered
- **Check settings**: Notifications enabled in Settings
- **Check email**: Correct email address in profile
- **Wait**: Can take a few minutes to arrive

### Map Not Loading

**Problem**: Location map doesn't show
- **Check internet**: Stable connection required
- **Allow location**: Enable browser location permission
- **Try**: Refresh the page
- **Try**: Different browser

### Admin Features Not Visible

**Problem**: Can't see admin dashboard or buttons
- **Check**: User has `is_admin = true` in database
- **Contact**: Site administrator to grant admin access
- **Try**: Log out and log back in

## Safety & Guidelines

### Food Safety

- Only share food that is **safe to eat**
- Check **expiration dates** before sharing
- Store food at **proper temperatures**
- Don't share food with **signs of spoilage**
- Include **allergen information** in listings
- Follow **local health regulations**

### Personal Safety

- Meet in **public locations** when possible
- Bring a **friend** if uncomfortable
- Share pickup details with **someone you trust**
- Trust your **instincts** - cancel if needed
- Report **suspicious activity** to admins

### Platform Guidelines

- Be **honest** in listings and claims
- Respect **community standards**
- No **discrimination** of any kind
- No **commercial sales** - sharing only
- Keep the platform **spam-free**
- **Report violations** to moderators

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Is DoGoods free to use?**
A: Yes! DoGoods is completely free for everyone.

**Q: How do I become an admin?**
A: Contact the platform administrator - admin access must be granted in the database.

**Q: Can I edit a listing after posting?**
A: Yes! Go to My Listings and click Edit on any of your posts.

**Q: What if someone doesn't show up for pickup?**
A: Mark the claim as cancelled and make it available again.

**Q: Can I share prepared/cooked food?**
A: Check local regulations - some areas allow it, others don't.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account (permanent action).

**Q: What happens to my data if I delete my account?**
A: Personal data is removed, but aggregated impact statistics remain anonymous.

**Q: Can I use DoGoods for my business?**
A: DoGoods is for personal food sharing, not commercial sales.

**Q: How are impact metrics calculated?**
A: Admins enter data, which is aggregated from all platform activity.

**Q: Can I share food from my restaurant?**
A: Yes! Many restaurants use DoGoods to share surplus food.
